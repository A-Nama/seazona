You wake up, but something feels... weird. First of all, you’re floating. Second, you can breathe underwater. Third, a fish wearing sunglasses just swam past you like this is totally normal.

Before you can freak out, you spot a massive underwater city in the distance. Neon coral, glowing sea creatures, and what looks like an octopus DJ spinning tracks at a shell-shaped club.

The turtle chuckles. "Ahh, fascinating answers... Perhaps you have more to offer this city than you think." With a flick of his fin, a shimmering wave of light pulses through the shop. You feel something shift— ✨Your true undersea self awakening.✨
